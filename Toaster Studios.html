§ 03 / The studio

One modder, one very serious cat.

Toaster Studios is a one-person mod shop with a cat on payroll. We started a few months ago and we ship slow on purpose.

The brief is short: crazy things in Minecraft that unbrain your brain. Physics jokes, tiny mechanics, the occasional regrettable mob — and if a YouTuber showed off a mod and never released it, we'll have a go at recreating it.

One mod is live so far. A few more are toasting. The cat supervises.

§ 04 / What we make

A short list, weird on purpose.

01

Cursed mechanics

One small, slightly broken-feeling idea, tuned until it's funny. Fabric, client + server.

02

YouTuber mod recreations

Saw a mod in a video and nobody ever shipped it? Send the link. We'll have a crack at it — that's how Jenga Mod happened.

03

Physics & minigames

Towers that fall, blocks that don't behave, party-style chaos. Stuff a server can actually play together.

04

Regrettable mobs

Tameable, untameable, or actively spiteful. If you have a sketch on a napkin we can probably model around it.

05

Suggestions, seriously

The whole studio is one person and a cat. Open an issue on GitHub, drop a Modrinth comment, whatever — we read them.

§ 05 / How we work

Proof. Mix. Toast. Plate.

01 / Proof

You send the link.

A YouTube clip, a screenshot, a half-finished idea typed at 2am. We write back with what we think it'd take.

02 / Mix

We prototype.

Couple of weekends of trying weird stuff in Fabric. Most of it goes in the bin. We show you the survivors.

03 / Toast

We build it properly.

Code, balance, sounds, permissions, the edge cases. The cat watches. This part is most of the project.

04 / Plate

Up on Modrinth.

Release notes, GitHub issues open, replies in the comments. If it breaks, we patch it. That's the deal.
